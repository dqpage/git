about

 

dQ is an innovation services firm with focus on building products in the web and mobile space. We specialize in interactive design, digital strategy and agile development abilities in creating cutting-edge digital platforms.

"Creativity is inventing, experimenting, growing, taking risks, breaking rules, making mistakes and having fun."

We love the challenges faced by disruptive startups, being overtly curious, exploring new possibilities and transforming ideas into meaningful products and services that can drive growth through technological innovation.

As we understand these challenges, we realize the importance of having an ecosystem that can validate the ideas, before they hit the local markets. Our forte lies in tinkering with the prototypes and pushing them reach a critical mass, with a passionate group that handles design, technology and digital strategy.

The future is digital and we intend to transform businesses dynamically

workflow
 

workflow

 

As we bake ideas into wonderful products, we ensure a systematic delivery routine that combines the best of design engineering and lean startup methodologies:

Step 1: Ideation - Classify ideas based on:
- that could become new companies
- that could become new products for existing companies
- that could become great new features or improve existing features for existing products

Step 2: Whiteboarding - Brainstorming with a whiteboard session

Step 3: Storyboarding - Transform whiteboard output into digital wireframes

Step 4: Mockups - Transform storyboard into Graphic Illustration/PSD mockup

Step 5: Design Engineering
- Front end development: PSD to Responsive Web/UI
- Back end development: Business logic and database

Step 6: Usability Testing - Design iteration and bug fixes

Step 7: Validate new solutions - Controlled experiments and real world pilots with MVP

Step 8: A/B Testing - Tweak, optimize and personalize

Step 9: Deployment - Web and mobile interfaces goes live

Step 10: Managed Services - Bug fixes, updates, SEO, content management, digital marketing
